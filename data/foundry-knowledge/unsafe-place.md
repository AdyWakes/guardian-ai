# Unknown Unsafe Place — Immediate Measures

Guidance for someone who feels unsafe but cannot identify a specific threat. Use when the user reports a general unsafe feeling or describes an unfamiliar environment.

## Steps

1. Move toward a public, well-lit, populated place. Distance from isolation is the priority.
2. Keep your phone unlocked and ready. Have your dial-emergency action one tap away.
3. Share your live location with a trusted contact if it is safe to do so.
4. Do not go to a private or isolated location, even if invited or offered shelter by a stranger.
5. If someone approaches or blocks you, call emergency services immediately. Make the call loudly so onlookers know you are seeking help.
6. Prepare an exit route. Keep distance from anyone behaving suspiciously and stay near other people whenever possible.
