# Being Followed — Immediate Actions

Guidance for someone who believes they are being followed in public.

## Steps

1. Stay in public, well-lit areas. Do not move toward isolated spots.
2. Do NOT go home — leading a follower to your residence puts you and your household at greater risk.
3. Head to a police station, fire station, hospital, or busy public business that has staff present.
4. Call emergency services (911 in the US, or your local equivalent).
5. Document the follower discreetly — physical description, clothing, vehicle make/model/color/plate, direction of travel.
6. Keep your phone charged, unlocked, and accessible. Share your live location with a trusted contact if it is safe to do so.
7. Tell someone your current location and where you are heading so they can check in on you.
