# Feeling Unsafe at a Bar or Club

Guidance for someone who feels unsafe inside a social venue such as a bar, club, lounge, or party.

## Steps

1. Locate venue security or bouncers immediately. They are trained to intervene and to remove threats.
2. Ask staff for help. Bartenders in most regions are trained to assist patrons who feel unsafe.
3. Use the "Angel Shot" code phrase if available at the venue. Ordering an Angel Shot signals to trained staff that you need help leaving safely.
4. Move to well-lit, populated areas inside the venue — never accept the offer of a quieter or more private spot.
5. Text your location and situation to friends. Include the venue name and the person making you uncomfortable if possible.
6. Do not leave alone. Wait for trusted transport, a friend, or for security to escort you out.
