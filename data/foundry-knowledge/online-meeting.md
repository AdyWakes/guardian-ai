# Unsafe Online Meeting

Guidance for someone meeting in person with a person they first met online — dating apps, social platforms, marketplace exchanges — where the situation has become uncomfortable.

## Steps

1. Always meet in public places. Coffee shops, busy restaurants, and shopping centers are appropriate; private residences and parking lots are not.
2. Tell someone your location, who you are meeting, the meeting time, and when you will check in afterward.
3. Have an exit plan. Arrange for a friend to call with a real or fake "emergency" at a pre-agreed time so you have an easy reason to leave.
4. Do not share personal information — home address, workplace, exact neighborhood, children's school — until you trust the person.
5. Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, leave immediately. Politeness is not worth your safety.
6. Drive yourself or use your own transport. Do not accept a ride from someone you just met; do not be left without a way to leave.
