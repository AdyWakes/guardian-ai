# Unsafe Transportation Situation

Guidance for someone who feels unsafe inside a vehicle — rideshare, taxi, bus, or with an unknown driver.

## Steps

1. Share your live location with a trusted contact immediately. Most rideshare apps and phones expose a one-tap location share.
2. Call emergency services discreetly if possible. Pretending to order food while actually calling 911 is a recognized technique that does not signal distress to the driver.
3. Note the vehicle make, model, color, and license plate. If you took a rideshare, capture the trip ID.
4. If safe, ask to be let out at a busy, public location — a gas station, hotel lobby, or 24-hour business.
5. Sit in the back seat behind the driver. This position makes it harder for the driver to reach you and easier for you to exit.
6. Trust your instincts. It is better to be rude or to lose a fare than to remain in danger.
