# Safe at Home Check-In

Guidance for someone who is safe at home and is checking in rather than reporting a threat. Used to confirm no emergency action is required.

## Steps

1. Keep your phone charged and nearby in case anything changes.
2. Lock doors and windows if that is normal for your situation.
3. Let a trusted contact know you are safe if someone may be worried about you.
4. Stay on standby. Reassess only if something specific changes — a new sound, an unexpected message, an unfamiliar visitor.
5. Do not send an emergency alert unless a new threat actually appears.
