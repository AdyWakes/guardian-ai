# Potential Home Intruder

Guidance for someone who suspects an intruder is in or attempting to enter their home.

## Steps

1. Stay quiet. Do not confront the intruder verbally or physically.
2. Call 911 immediately. Whisper if you must, or use silent text-to-911 where available.
3. Lock yourself in a room with a sturdy door. A bathroom or bedroom with a deadbolt is ideal.
4. Stay on the line with emergency services until officers arrive. Do not hang up to "check."
5. Do NOT hide in closets. They are common search points and offer no exit.
6. If you must flee, prioritize known exits. Avoid passing rooms where the intruder may be.
7. Have an escape route planned in advance, with a designated rally point outside the home.
