# General Safety Precautions

Baseline everyday safety guidance for users who are not in an active emergency but want a low-risk safety check-in.

## Steps

1. Stay aware of your surroundings. Limit phone-fixation when walking or in unfamiliar areas.
2. Keep your phone charged. A dead phone removes your fastest line to help.
3. Share your location with trusted contacts when going out, especially at night or to new places.
4. Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, treat it as wrong and act on it.
5. Have emergency contacts easily accessible — pinned to your home screen, on a lock-screen widget, or written down.
6. Know the locations of nearby safe places: police stations, fire stations, hospitals, 24-hour businesses.
